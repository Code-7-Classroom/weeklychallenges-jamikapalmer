About Us

Born from a need for a wider clothing selction for boys, my two baby boys have inspired me to prove that fun, fashionable boys' clothing is not only possible, but also affordable! For the Boys aims to bring color, joy, and style to every young boy's wardrobe. The brand captures the magic of childhood through high quality basic clothing, featured in beautiful colors that is both fashionable, timeless and now available within budget to become a stylish staple to parents throughout the world.
